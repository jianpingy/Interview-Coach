Job Title: Data Scientist
Location: Hybrid – New York, NY (3 days onsite per week) Employment Type: Full-time Department: Data & Analytics

About the Role We are seeking a highly motivated Data Scientist to join our growing Analytics team. You’ll work closely with data engineers, product managers, and business stakeholders to design, build, and deploy data-driven solutions that power key decisions across the organization.
Your work will combine statistical modeling, machine learning, and experimentation to uncover actionable insights and improve product and business outcomes.

Key Responsibilities - Develop, validate, and deploy predictive and prescriptive models using Python or R. - Analyze large, complex datasets to identify trends, patterns, and opportunities for optimization. - Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define key metrics and evaluate business strategies through A/B testing and causal inference. - Build automated pipelines for data collection, cleaning, and feature engineering. - Present findings and recommendations to technical and non-technical stakeholders through clear visualizations and storytelling. - Stay current with new tools, techniques, and best practices in machine learning, data science, and AI.

Required Qualifications - Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Applied Mathematics, or a related field. - 2–5 years of experience in a data science or machine learning role. - Proficiency in Python (NumPy, pandas, scikit-learn, PyTorch or TensorFlow). - Experience with SQL and data querying in large-scale data environments (Snowflake, BigQuery, Redshift, etc.). - Strong understanding of statistics, hypothesis testing, and experimental design. - Experience with data visualization tools such as Tableau, Power BI, or Plotly.

Preferred Qualifications - Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, Azure) and containerization (Docker). - Background in NLP, recommender systems, or time series forecasting. - Experience deploying machine learning models in production. - Familiarity with MLOps and version control (Git, MLflow).

Why Join Us - Competitive salary and performance-based bonuses. - Comprehensive health, dental, and vision coverage. - 401(k) with company match. - Annual learning stipend and access to online training resources. - Opportunity to work on impactful projects in a collaborative, data-driven environment.
